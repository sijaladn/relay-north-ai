# Relay North AI Brand Notes

## Working Name

Relay North AI

This name fits the offer because "Relay" suggests moving knowledge and work through a clearer system, while "North" gives the brand a Toronto/Canada signal without sounding provincial. I did a quick web search and did not find an obvious exact-name conflict for "Relay North AI", but this is not legal clearance. Confirm domain and trademark availability before paid launch.

## Positioning

Practical AI training and workflow automation for Toronto SMBs.

## Core Offer

Relay North AI helps teams use AI for presentations, documents, reporting, workflows, and internal operations. Training is the low-friction entry point; focused workflow systems become the paid implementation path.
